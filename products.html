An eligible person may be admitted into the membership of the Sacco if he or she possesses the following qualifications:

a) Is within the categories of membership i. Original members who signed the application
for registration including but not limited to being members of CUEA staff, its affiliate,
and constituent colleges; Sacco staff; CUEA Students; or Kenyans living in Diaspora

b) Is a spouse or child of the members admitted under a) above;

c) Is at least (18) years of age;

d) Is not carrying out such activities detrimental to the objectives of the Sacco;

e) Is not directly involved in money lending that can be perceived to cause a conflict of interest;

f) Has regular income from employment, business or trade, or any other legitimate source; and

g) Has accepted the objectives of the Sacco, the obligations of members, and other conditions as
stated in the Sacco by-Laws and this membership policy.
